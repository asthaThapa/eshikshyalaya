About Eshikshyalaya

What is E-shikshyalaya?

Eshikshyalaya is an online platform for student assignment management system that allows student to submit their assignment or project online without any physical file. Before the submission, the student needs to update their progress to the system and the lecturer will able to review the submitted assignments. It provides platform for document sharing for student and lecturer along with other facilities such as view and sharing of notes that has been used for recent classes. It will be able to make the managing of assignments easier as every assignment will be recorded at the same time can be review later when required
